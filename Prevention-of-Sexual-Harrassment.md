# Prevention of Sexual Harassment

### Question 1
What kinds of behaviour cause sexual harassment?

#### 1. Physical Harassment
* Severe Acts
* Inappropriate Touching
* Forced Intimacy
* Physical Obstruction

#### 2. Verbal Harassment
* Sexually colored Remarks
* Personal Questions
* Requests or Demands
* Teasing Sounds
* Demeaning Terms

#### 3. Visual Harassment
* Digital Harassment
* Sexual Gestures
* Sharing Explicit Content
* Hostile Staring

#### 4. Quid Pro Quo
* Threats of Detrimental Treatment
* Promises of Preferential Treatment

---

### Question 2
What would you do in case you face or witness any incident or repeated incidents of such behaviour?

#### If I Face Harassment Personally:
* I will speak up Immediately
* Document every detail
* Preserve Digital Evidence
* I will file a formal complaint with Internal Committee

#### If I Witness Harassment Happening to others:
* I use 5D's of Bystander Intervention:
  * Distract
  * Delegate
  * Document
  * Delay
  * Direct
* Offer my support as a witness
* I will report what I saw

#### If repeated incidents of such behaviour:
* I will report to IC
* Seek escalation if needed
* I will use SHe-Box if IC fails
